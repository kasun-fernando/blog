--- layout: home search_exclude: true --- ### **I am a mathematician, math educator, and a machine learning & data science enthusiast**. ### Currently, I am a **Junior Visitor in Dynamical Systems** at the [Centro di Ricerca Matematica Ennio De Giorgi](http://www.crm.sns.it/), Scuola Normale Superiore di Pisa in Italy. ### I completed my PhD in Mathematics at the University of Maryland in the US under the supervision of [Dmitry Dolgopyat](https://www.math.umd.edu/~dolgop/) in August 2018 with the [dissertation](https://drum.lib.umd.edu/handle/1903/21402): *Higher Order Asymptotics for the Central Limit Theorem and Large Deviation Principles*. Afterwards, from August 2018 to July 2021, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Toronto in Canada where I continued my [**research**](https://kasun-fernando.github.io/personal-webpage/Research/) work in **chaotic dynamical systems** & **Markov processes**, and branched out into **inferential statistics** & **machine learning**. ### Apart from research work, I am actively involved in the mathematical community as an educator. Due to my commitment to [**teaching**](https://kasun-fernando.github.io/personal-webpage/Teaching/), in 2020, I won the *Award for Teaching Excellence by a Junior Member* of the Mathematics Department, University of Toronto, Mississauga. Also, since the beginning of my undergraduate studies, I have been contributing to many [**outreach**](https://kasun-fernando.github.io/personal-webpage/Outreach/) projects in Canada, Sri Lanka, and the US with the hope of popularizing mathematics among the general public and promoting diversity in STEM. ### [**Here**](otherfiles/KasunFernandoCV.pdf) is my complete CV. ### During my free time, I like to travel and do photography.
